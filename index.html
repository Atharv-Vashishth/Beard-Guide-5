90-Day Beard Growth Plan

Follow this step-by-step 10-point routine and check progress every 2 weeks.
Save this — repeat for 90 days. Designed by Atharv Vashishth
1
Start with a baseline
Take clear photos (front + both sides). Track progress every 2 weeks — visual records keep you accountable and motivated.
2
Clean your face daily
Morning & night: use a mild cleanser to unclog pores and maintain follicle health. Avoid harsh soaps that strip natural oils.
3
Exfoliate twice a week
Removes dead skin and helps new hairs push through faster. Use a gentle scrub or natural mix (coffee + honey).
4
Moisturize + light beard oil
Healthy skin = healthy beard. Apply a few drops of light oil to the skin and hair tips to lock moisture without greasiness.
5
Massage daily (1–2 mins)
Use fingertips or a soft brush — boosts blood flow and wakes up dormant follicles.
6
Let it grow for 4–8 weeks
Avoid trimming early. Shape lightly after the first month to define your neckline and keep the silhouette neat.
7
Eat for growth
Prioritise protein, eggs, nuts, spinach, salmon and hydrate well. Sleep 7–8 hours — recovery = growth.
8
Optional supplements
If you’re low on nutrients: consider biotin, zinc, omega-3 or vitamin D. Always check with a doctor before starting supplements.
9
Stay consistent (90 days)
Take weekly photos, stick to the routine, and avoid constantly switching products — consistency compounds results.
10
If no progress after 6 months
See a dermatologist — they can recommend clinical options like topical treatments or further investigation.
⚡ Bonus Tips
Don’t expect overnight change — beard growth is patience + consistency. Avoid harsh chemicals, over-washing, and keep stress low (high cortisol slows hair growth).
